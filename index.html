About Angel

Creative technologist, artist, and explorer.

"Hi, I’m Angel — a builder, storyteller, and community-driven creator who’s obsessed with helping people find clarity, meaning, and support in the moments they need it most. I’ve spent the past few years moving between worlds: winning Hack the North with a health-tech app, living as a digital nomad across 10+ countries, growing a YouTube channel to 9,000 subscribers, and creating content that reached over 700,000 views. Along the way, I worked as a Product Designer at Hootsuite and later transitioned into Growth + GTM roles for early-stage startups, where I discovered my love for building communities, designing thoughtful experiences, and turning ideas into real products that people feel seen by. Today, my work lives at the intersection of psychology, women’s health, and neurodivergence. I care deeply about human flourishing, self-discovery, and creating spaces (digital or in-person) where people feel supported, understood, and connected. Whether it’s designing an app, hosting a circle, or sharing my own journey, my goal is always the same: to make the world feel a bit more spacious, soft, and empowering for people who think and feel deeply. If you’re building something aligned, exploring purpose, or simply curious — I’d love to connect."
